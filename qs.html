Questionable Skies

Make your own world in this Minecraft modpack.

You Make the World

In this modpack you spawn in the sky, forcing you to create the world around you. This gives you the freedom to create whatever you please without needing to dig out space.

Discover New Lands

Just because the overworld is gone doesn't mean the rest are. Get your exploration fix with the enhanced Nether or End dimensions. With new biomes, blocks and mobs there's a lot more to these biomes than you'd expect.

Create Advanced Machines

There's a plethora of machinery to create. Use the power of kinetic energy to create contraptions like an automatic metal sifter or a tree harvester. Or go the nuclear route and create fission reactors and steam-powered drones.

Magic

With the additions of mods like Botania there's no shortage of magic for you to play with. You can cast spells, craft new gear and travel to new dimensions, and even learn how to fly.

Multiplayer

With built in multiplayer support, up to 8 groups can play on the same world at a time. With customized islands and a main hub island to explore each group will have a different experience. You can have as many people as you want in a group. Each group shares progress including quests, and you can set up trades between groups. It's more fun with friends, so bring a few.
