Privacy Policy

This notice expains how and when PixelChunk, pixelchunk.net and its related services collect personal information, what we do with it and your right to see or change it.

Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information from you, including information about your computer or network.

This information is used to provide our services, including but not limited to:
• Connecting to our game servers
• Providing a network connection to our website

Besides our staff, we also share this information with:
• Epic Games, who host our online services,
• Google, to serve our domain and website, and
• Github, who hosts our website, including this privacy policy.

To use our services, you must log into your Epic Games account and provide your IP address.

Request a Copy or Correction of your Data

We do not hold any personal information about you, however the 3rd party services we use might.
We recommend reading their privacy policies if you would like to know what information they store about you.
You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong.
If you would like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at data@pixelchunk.net.
