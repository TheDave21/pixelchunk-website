Experience the newest form of strategy and sneakery in the sequel to Chess.

Bigger Board, Bigger Games

The larger board with more pieces creates longer, more strategic gameplay. It also helps hide your King.

New Pieces

Chess 2 has brand new pieces, like the pike! You will also discover new ones throughout your games, some of which no player controls, and even some both players control.

Random Events

New random events make every game unique, and reduces the skill gap for casual play. You can turn them on or off as you please.

Chaos Mode

If you really want a challenge, try chaos mode. Extreme random events, even more pieces and even changes to the board will spice up the game.

Clubs

Join a club and compete in club tournaments. Rank up your club on the global leaderboard, discuss tactics, challenge your friends and play friendly competitions. With a club, you unlock features that enhance your experience.
